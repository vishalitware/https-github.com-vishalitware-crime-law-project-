Leave granted.
The appellant approached this Court aggrieved by the denial of protection under Section 438 Cr.P.C. During the pendency of the appeal before us, we are informed that the police has completed their
investigation and has filed report under Section 173(2) Cr.P.C. The appellant is shown as an accused. In the above circumstances, this appeal is disposed of granting a period of one month from today
to the appellant to surrender before the Trial Court and seek regular bail, in which case the application of the appellant may be considered on the same day, on its own merits. Learned counsel appearing for the appellantsubmits that in the case of the co-accused, bail has
already been granted.It will be open to the appellant to point out the same before the Trial Court. Pending applications, if any, shall stand disposed of.
